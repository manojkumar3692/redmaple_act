Heavy Construction Equipment

ACT represents a wide range of heavy construction equipment manufacturers in South India. Some of its principals are Volvo, Epiroc and so on.

ACT’s heavy construction equipment range include excavators, loaders, graders, pavers, crushers and any other large equipment that are found on a construction site.

Volvo Construction Equipment is one of the world’s largest manufacturers of construction machines. Its product portfolio includes wheel loaders, backhoe loaders, excavators, articulated haulers, motor graders, skid steers, pavers and compactors. Each and every machine comes with the quality, performance and safety one expects from the Volvo name. ACT represents Volvo Construction Equipment in Tamilnadu and Kerala providing 24/7 immediate parts and service support.

Epiroc is a world leading provider of industrial productivity solutions. ACT represents Atlascopco in Tamilnadu and Kerala for its compressed air and gas equipment, construction and mining equipment.

Powerscreen is a branch under the powerful global brand Terex Pegson. Powerscreen is an expert in the design and manufacture of mobile crushing, screening, washing and recycling equipment. Their range of machines operate in the toughest environments and feature nominal set-up times, market leading productivity rates and excellent reliability figures. ACT represents Powerscrees in Tamilnadu.
