Penta Agreement signed

The Dealership agreement was signed between Volvo Penta & Pact Power Solutions LLP at Volvo head office in Bangalore on 1-Dec-2016. The photos show Mr. Jonas Nilsson – Vice President, Volvo Penta, India alongwith Mr. Mohan Ramanathan & Mr. Thomas Martin. J, Pact Power Solutions LLP during the signoff. The territory of the dealership will be Tamil Nadu & Pondicherry. The major business units in Volvo Penta as below : Industrial engines ( includes offroad engines, genset engines ) · Marine engines & drive systems ( includes leisure & commercial ) Volvo Penta has been in India from 1996 & Volvo Penta engines will also be manufactured from Pithampur plant, India from April,2017. This new business line of Volvo Penta will expand the existing business portfolio of ACT presently comprising of Volvo Construction equipments, Volvo Road Machines, Epiroc into further new segments.
